POULTRY

Poultry refers to any domesticated bird used for food. This includes a wide range of birds, such as chicken, turkey, goose, duck, Rock Cornish hens, and game birds such as pheasant, squab and guinea fowl. Huge birds such as ostrich, emu and rhea (ratites) are also included. Poultry can be raised for eggs, meat and/or feathers. Chickens, ducks, turkeys, and geese are of primary commercial importance.
